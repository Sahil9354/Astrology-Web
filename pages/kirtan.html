About Kirtan

Kirtan is a sacred devotional practice where the names and glories of God are sung collectively with music, rhythm, and devotion. It is a powerful way to purify the mind and create spiritual upliftment.

Our kirtan services include traditional bhajans, naam-sankirtan, and interactive devotional singing that fills the environment with divine vibrations.

Kirtan can be organized for temples, homes, satsangs, festivals, spiritual gatherings, and special religious occasions.
